About RSR Global

RSR Global is a leading group of companies specializing in recruitment and staffing solutions across diverse industries, including Healthcare, Hospitality, IT, Engineering, and Banking & Finance. With a global presence spanning 5 continents and a network across 130+ locations, we have successfully relocated over 1,700 individuals worldwide in 2023 alone.
